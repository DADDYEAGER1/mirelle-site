## Understanding Tomato Red (The Color Science)

<strong>Orange-red manicure</strong> execution begins with pigment composition. Tomato red contains higher orange pigment ratio than classic red. On color wheel, it positions between pure red and orange—leaning red but influenced by adjacent warmth. This chromatic placement creates distinctive appearance: brighter than burgundy, warmer than crimson, more sophisticated than coral.

The tomato descriptor references specific hue matching ripe tomato color at peak ripeness. Not cherry tomato brightness. Not underripe tomato's orange dominance. Fully mature tomato presents balanced orange-based red—warm without crossing into overtly orange territory. This precision distinguishes tomato red from coral-red neighbors on spectrum.
