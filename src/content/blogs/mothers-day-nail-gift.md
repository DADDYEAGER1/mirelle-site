## How to Choose the Right Nail Gift Based on Her Experience Level

This is the question that trips most gift-buyers up, and the answer is simpler than it looks.

If she's never done her own nails at home, start her off with something complete and non-intimidating — a beginner nail drill gift box or a lamp-and-polish kit that includes everything she needs without requiring any existing knowledge. If she already has a UV/LED lamp, do not buy her another one. A standalone <strong>gel nail polish set</strong> is a far more considered choice, and it signals that you actually paid attention.

For the mum who's been doing her own nails for a while and has the basics covered, the right move is an upgrade — a more powerful rechargeable drill, a professional-tier e-file, or a larger polish collection in colours she wouldn't necessarily buy herself.
